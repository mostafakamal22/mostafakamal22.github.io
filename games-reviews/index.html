Game Details

Title: One Punch Man: World

Category: Action Game
Platform: Windows
Status: Live

One Punch Man: World is a free-to-play multiplayer 3D action game from Crunchyroll Games. Players take on the role of characters from the popular anime including Saitama, Mumen, Silverfand, Rider, and Genos, and fight against some well-known villains. Each character boasts their own fighting style and unique set of abilities, just like in the anime. In keeping with that, players can expect cinematic finishing moves. Challenge monsters with a friend via the Hero Association HQ hub. It’s more fun than doing it alone. The game also offers players plenty of story to get invested in even allowing a peek at the characters’ daily lives, including more mundane things like shopping at the market. Available on Android, iOS, and PC, One Punch Man: World also features cross-play and progression.
